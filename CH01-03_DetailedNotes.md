  - Problem: The Budapest moment (lines 151–171) is where the narrator tells Julia to see other men.

  - Why this matters: The book's credibility depends on whether the narrator takes responsibility

  - Problem: The chapter jumps between several timeframes: Cyprus (the main narrative), references

  - Resolution path: Either tighten the timeline (only reference what's essential to understanding
  Cyprus) or use clearer temporal markers ("A year after Cyprus, at a Christmas party..." instead of

  - Why this matters: If the book is meant to show Julia's complexity and agency, she needs her own
  voice, not just the narrator's interpretation of her body. Her trauma deserves to be shown, not

  - Why this matters: If the book is deconstructing male delusion, this language should feel

  - Problem: The Uber driver section (lines 59–97) is so powerful that it consumes the emotional

  driver story is told as exposition and the others are shown, the reader's attention gets pulled

  - Why this matters: If Cyprus is supposed to be the emotional anchor of the chapter (the moment
  Julia fell in love), that should be the climactic moment, not an epilogue to the Uber driver

  - Why this matters: The book's credibility depends on whether the narrator takes responsibility

  - Problem: The chapter jumps between several timeframes: Cyprus (the main narrative), references

  - Resolution path: Either tighten the timeline (only reference what's essential to understanding
  Cyprus) or use clearer temporal markers ("A year after Cyprus, at a Christmas party..." instead of

  - Why this matters: If the book is meant to show Julia's complexity and agency, she needs her own
  voice, not just the narrator's interpretation of her body. Her trauma deserves to be shown, not

  - Why this matters: If the book is deconstructing male delusion, this language should feel

  - Problem: The Uber driver section (lines 59–97) is so powerful that it consumes the emotional

  - Why this matters: If Cyprus is supposed to be the emotional anchor of the chapter (the moment
