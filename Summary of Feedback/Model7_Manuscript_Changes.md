## THEMATIC PRIORITIES (Model 7 Positioning)

### Make Central to Every Chapter:
1. **Power dynamics** (who has it, who's gaining/losing it)
2. **Desire** (sexual, emotional, financial attraction)
3. **Choice** (Julia's agency throughout, not victim status)
4. **Sexuality** (erotic but not gratuitous, central to meaning)
5. **Sophistication** (literary quality, psychological depth)

### Tone Targets:
- Sensual, not crude
- Complex, not simple
- Psychologically authentic, not voyeuristic
- Julia empowered, not victimized
- Narrator unreliable, not villainous (even though he IS controlling)

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### Julia's Voice
- By end of revision, Julia should have POV in at least 5-7 chapters
- Ch09 is template—maintain that quality elsewhere
- Reader should UNDERSTAND her experience, not just narrator's interpretation

### Power Dynamics
- Every sex scene should show power negotiation (explicit or implicit)
- Narrator's control should be clear but Julia's resistance/compliance should be choice
- Final choice (Italian) should be inevitable based on power shift

### Climax Drama
- Ch15 is MAKE-OR-BREAK
- If not fully dramatized, entire non-linear structure falls apart
- Reader needs to WITNESS Julia's choice, not read reflection about it

### Erotic Positioning
- Explicit content should be *integrated* to meaning, not tacked on
- Each sex scene should reveal character/relationship, not just be hot
- Goal: Literary erotica, not erotica with literary pretensions
